About

Mobile App Developer, 3D Animator, Video Editor and Martial Artist! Having studied in Bristol and London for the past 4 years, gives me a broad experience in a range of skills that I picked up, my goal is to introduce innovative ideas that adds the coolness factor in every project I do, I also tend to think outside the box. Ever since my last visit to Hong Kong in 2018, I started to take keen interest in Martial Arts in general, even though I'm not competitive but I've trained in Martial Arts such as Kung Fu, Silat & Tae Kwon-Do, giving me an all-rounded edge, my other interests are cycling, working out, snooker and travelling the world. YES I created this site with my bare hands, though not perfect at web coding, just to show you how multi-talented I'am ;)



Get Started!
3D MA: Martial Arts App series
Combining my love for app development, 3D animation and martial arts, I produced an app for users to views moves and techniques from all angles, not so much of a beginner's guide but this will definetely spread awareness of other styles.
Pakistan Zindabad!
A 3D animated documentary-style short of how Pakistan was created and the uprising of it's founded, Muhammad Ali Jinnah, this was also apart of my MSc dissertation
The Jinn: Unity game
A Superhero game I produced while studying for my MSc based on a concept from 10 years ago, the game is a homage to the classic PS2 games with a modern twist, it also features me as the main character of the game.
Virtua Fighter 6 Trailer
A fanmade 3D animated trailer paying tribute to the classic fighting game franchise, using the university's mocap studio to bring life to the characters, using my knowledge in Bajiquan style of kung fu, I mocapped the main protagonist Akira Yuki along with another colleague of mine who mocapped the main boss.
Just Doing My Job!
A Martial Arts Action short that I did for my MSc, blend sound design with visuals, making this a kickass experience for anyone to watch.
MindBox
A short dystopian film that advertises fictional futuristic products, inspired by Black Mirror, this product hacks security systems that spies on people 24/7. This project was produced during my third-year undergraduate degree and it showcases my VFX skills.
